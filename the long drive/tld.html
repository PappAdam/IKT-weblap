The Long Drive

Introduction

This is a car tour game in an almost endless randomly generated desert. The point is freedom, leadership, discovery and immersion. Includes minimal car maintenance and surviving elements.

About the game

There are no invisible walls or impenetrable low fences or flashing red "wrong direction" signs on the display. The car will not consume 10 to 20 gallons of gasoline for a few hundred meters, with a full tank, you could go about 500 kilometers. If you see a big mountain in the distance, you can go there and if you can, you can go up on it.
